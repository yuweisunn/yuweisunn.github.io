Research Interests

Biography

I am a Ph.D. student in the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology at the University of Tokyo. I received B.Sc. in Computer Science and Technology in 2018 from North China Electric Power University and M.Sc. in Information and Communication Engineering with honors in 2021 from the University of Tokyo. In 2020, I was the fellow of the Advanced Study Program (ASP) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. I have been working with the United Nations University on Cybersecurity since 2019. I am also a member of the AI Security and Privacy Team at the RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project and a research fellow at the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science. I visited UPenn, MIT, Waseda, and Chula.
