Research Interests

    I am interested in Artificial Intelligence, Cyber-Physical Systems, and Network Systems.

Biography

    Yuwei Sun is a Ph.D.’s student in the Department of Information Science and Technology at the University of Tokyo. He received B.E. in Computer Science and Technology in 2018 from North China Electric Power University. In 2020, he was the fellow of the ASP at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. Since 2019, he has been working with the Campus Computing Centre at the United Nations University Centre on Intelligent Networking Systems.
