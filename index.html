Research Interests

I am interested in Deep Learning, Trustworthy AI, and Cybersecurity.


Biography

Yuwei Sun is a PhD’s student in the Graduate School of Information Science and Technology at the University of Tokyo. He received B.E. in Computer Science and Technology in 2018 from North China Electric Power University and M.E. in Information and Communication Engineering with honors in 2021 from the University of Tokyo. In 2020, he was the fellow of the Advanced Study Program (ASP) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. He has been working with the Campus Computing Centre at the United Nations University Centre on Cybersecurity since 2019. He is a member of the AI Security and Privacy Team at the RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project working on trustworthy AI, and a research fellow at Japan Society for the Promotion of Science (JSPS).
